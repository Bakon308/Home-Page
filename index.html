The White Tiger serves as Grimoria's foremost institution for civil order, magic law enforcement, and intelligence coordination — operating across all ring classifications to ensure the safety of every citizen within the city's walls.

Our Mission

To uphold the laws of the Atellian Empire within the jurisdiction of Grimoria; to investigate incidents involving unauthorized magic use, civil disruption, and threats to the city's four foundational institutions.

Jurisdiction

The White Tiger maintains primary jurisdiction over all wards of Grimoria, extending to the bridge corridors and mountain passes that serve as entry points into the city. Coordination with the Bronze Guard is maintained for cross-border matters.

Ring Neutrality

Officers of the White Tiger are bound by the City Charter to enforce the law without prejudice toward ring classification. All citizens — from White to Gold — are subject to equal investigation and due process.

The Four Pillars

The White Tiger operates in coordination with the city's four foundational institutions: the Black Tortoise Academy, the Vermillion Bird Medical Authority, the Azure Dragon Enterprise, and this division itself.

Bridge Corridor — Train Disruption & Explosion

A detonation event occurred aboard a passenger service arriving from Hurst during the afternoon of June 24. The train came to rest on the bridge spanning the lake perimeter. Injuries were minor; no civilian fatalities confirmed. ████████ individuals fell from the train into the lake. Investigation is ongoing. Witnesses are urged to contact Force 1.

24 Jun

Unexplained Fire Fatalities — Civilian & Detained

Two separate incidents of fatal spontaneous combustion have been recorded, affecting a civilian in the Central Market district and a detained individual held at White Tiger HQ. Both events display characteristics inconsistent with conventional fire magic. A specialist team has been assigned. Citizens are advised to report any red-coated individuals to Force 1 immediately.
